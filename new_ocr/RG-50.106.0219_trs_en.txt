PREFACE

The following interview is part of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's
collection of oral testimonies. Rights to the interview are held by the United States Holocaust
Memorial Museum.

The reader should bear in mind that this is a verbatim transcript of spoken, rather than
written prose. This transcript has been neither checked for spelling nor verified for accuracy, and
therefore, it is possible that there are errors. As a result, nothing should be quoted or used from this
transcript without first checking it against the taped interview.

Rose Liberman: Rose Liberman.
Q: And what the name that you were born with? Your maiden name?
A: Szternberg.

A: I was born in Poland.

Q: Let’s talk a little bit about your family. Your parents. What were their names?
